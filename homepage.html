About Emo

Our Mission and Vision

Founded in 2015, Emo is a non-profitable organization that is dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for Babondo communities facing poverty, hunger, and lack of access to education. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive, and we work towards this vision through comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.

Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by addressing its root causes. We partner with local communities to develop programs that are culturally sensitive and sustainable in the long term. Through our work, we've already impacted over 100,000 lives across 15 countries.

We are proud to have earned a 4-star rating from Charity Navigator for our financial transparency and program effectiveness, ensuring that your donations are used to maximum impact.
