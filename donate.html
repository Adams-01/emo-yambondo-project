Payment Instructions

MTN MoMo (Uganda)

  1. Dial **\*165\*3#** (Pay for Goods & Services).
  2. Select **Option 1: Merchant Code**.
  3. Enter our **Merchant Code/ID**: NGO_AID256.
  4. Enter the exact amount: UGX [Amount].
  5. Enter the **Reference/Reason**: **DONATE**.
  6. Confirm the transaction with your **Mobile Money PIN**.

Airtel Money (Uganda)

  1. Dial **\*185\*9#** (Pay Merchant).
  2. Enter our **Merchant Number/Till ID**: 900888 (Example).
  3. Enter the exact amount: UGX [Amount].
  4. Enter the **Reference/Reason**: **DONATE**.
  5. Confirm the transaction with your **Airtel Money PIN**.

Vodacom M-Pesa (Cross-Border from Tanzania/Kenya)

As an M-Pesa user outside Uganda, you can send money directly to our Ugandan number via cross-border services.

  1. Use your M-Pesa Menu to select **"Send Money Overseas"** (or similar option).
  2. Select the receiving country: **Uganda**.
  3. Select the receiving network: **MTN Uganda** or **Airtel Money**.
  4. Enter our NGO's number: +25677/075XXXXXX.
  5. Enter the equivalent amount in your local currency.
  6. Confirm the transaction with your **M-Pesa PIN**.

You will receive an SMS confirmation when the payment is successful. **Do not close this page until payment is complete.**
