Meet Our Team

Jules Asende

Co-founder

“Throughout my journey as a refugee and even now, countless individuals have touched my life in meaningful and inspiring ways, in the refugee settlements, in meetings, and schools, shaping the person I am today. I carry no excuse but a deep responsibility to pay these gifts forward to my community. My mission, therefore, is to live a healthy, helpful, and happy life, empowering others to overcome adversity and thrive.”
